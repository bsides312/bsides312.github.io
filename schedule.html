Schedule Information

All sessions will take place at the Irish American Heritage Center. The Blue Line track is in the Main Auditorium, and the Red Line track is in Classroom 304.

Breaks with refreshments will be available between sessions in the main hallway.

For accessibility accommodations, please speak with a BSides312 staff member (identifiable by their staff t-shirts) or email accessibility@bsides312.org.

BSides312 is Chicago's Biggest Little Non-Profit hacking & information security conference, run by and for the local community.
